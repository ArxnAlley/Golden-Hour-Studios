Our standard photography sessions are priced based on the duration and type of event.

Hourly rates start at $170/hour for single sessions.

Longer sessions can be customized to fit your schedule and needs.

We offer flexible booth setups for all events. Choose from classic, themed, or open-air booths. Booth changes during the event can be accommodated for an additional fee of $50 per change.

Special event packages include weddings, birthdays, formals, reunions, graduations, newborn shoots, corporate parties, and private celebrations.

Wedding coverage starts at $1200 for up to 5 hours, with optional add-ons such as photo albums, extra prints, or on-site editing.

Corporate events start at $500 for up to 3 hours, including digital delivery of all images.

Birthday parties start at $350 for up to 2 hours, with optional photo prints or digital albums.

Formals (proms, dances) start at $400 for up to 3 hours, including group and solo shots.

Reunions start at $450 for up to 3 hours, with group photos and digital delivery.

Graduations start at $300 for up to 2 hours, covering ceremonies and portraits.

Newborn shoots start at $400 for up to 2 hours, including basic props and digital images.

Private celebrations start at $350 for up to 2 hours, with custom coverage per client request.

Additional services include professional lighting setups, props, green screen backgrounds, and instant social media sharing options.

Our team is happy to tailor packages for any special occasion, ensuring you get a memorable experience without exceeding your budget.
